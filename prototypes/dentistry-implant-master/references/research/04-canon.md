# Track 04 — Canon (implant dentistry)

> Wave 1 Track 04 — essential books / consensus statements / RCTs / systematic reviews / Chinese textbooks / 反面 canon. Industry: implant dentistry (osseointegrated dental implants — diagnosis, site preparation, surgical placement, prosthetic restoration, peri-implant maintenance; NOT 普通牙体牙髓 / 正畸 / 儿牙 / 颌面肿瘤). Locale=global (英文一手为主体, 中文 ≈ 1:3-1:4 厚度), profile=practitioner (oral surgeon / periodontist / prosthodontist / implant-focused GP).
>
> 结构约束 notes (诚实): (1) 口腔种植 canon 90% 是 1985 (Brånemark 奠基) → ITI Treatment Guides Vol 1-12 + Buser GBR + Tarnow esthetics + 2017 World Workshop peri-implant classification 这一条主线, 必读浓度极高, 几乎所有 ITI/AO Fellow 都能背出半数; (2) 经典 RCT + systematic review 大部分进 PubMed/PMC 公开 abstract + 部分 free full-text (PMC + JCP open access + COIR rolling open), 一手率天然高; (3) 教科书 (Quintessence / Wiley / Elsevier) 出版社 listing 都是 surrogate_primary + vendor docs note; ITI/AO/EAO 协会立场文件全部 surrogate_primary + association note; (4) 中文教科书厚度有限 — 林野《口腔种植学》、宿玉成《现代口腔种植学》、邱立新《口腔种植学》是国内 3 大主流, 另有周磊数字化与华西满毅复杂骨增量, 走 人卫出版社 / book.douban.com 主域 + 大学医院教研页, **严禁** mp.weixin.qq.com 公众号 (即使是林野 / 宿玉成本人发表) — 改走学会 + 大学医院 + 期刊; (5) 反面 canon: Esposito Cochrane 系列长期揭厂商赞助 RCT bias + Klokkevold/Han 2007 风险因素诚实复盘; (6) 部分中文教材在 book.douban.com 主域无条目, 仅 m. 子域 (m.douban.com), 改用 人卫出版社 (pmphmedicine.com) 官方 listing 作 surrogate_primary 兜底.

### 必读教科书 — 全球奠基 (founding textbooks 1985-2024)

- **Brånemark+Zarb+Albrektsson 1985 — *Tissue-Integrated Prostheses: Osseointegration in Clinical Dentistry*** (Quintessence). 现代口腔种植学创世记。Per-Ingvar Brånemark (Gothenburg, 瑞典) 与 George Zarb (Toronto), Tomas Albrektsson (Gothenburg) 把 1965-1985 二十年 osseointegration 基础研究 + 临床随访集结成册, 定义了 *titanium-bone direct structural and functional connection* 概念 + two-stage submerged protocol + Lekholm-Zarb 骨质骨量分类。**所有现代种植 protocol 的源头**, ITI Fellow / AO Fellow 必读。今天 Brånemark protocol 即"延期种植 + 二期手术 + 3-6 月负荷"虽已被 immediate loading + 即刻种植替代部分场景, 但作为基线参考永不褪色。[T04-S001] [T04-S002] [T04-S003] [T04-S004] [T04-S006]

- **Misch 2008 / Resnik 2020 — *Contemporary Implant Dentistry* 3rd ed / *Misch's Contemporary Implant Dentistry* 4th ed** (Elsevier/Saunders). Carl E. Misch (ICOI 创始人之一, 美国种植教育核心, 2017 逝世) 把美国 prosthetics-driven implant 思路体系化, 提出 D1-D4 骨密度分类 + bone-graded surgical protocol + restoration-driven 治疗设计。4th ed (Randolph Resnik 接棒, 2020) 全面更新数字化 + zygomatic + All-on-X。**美国种植教育中坚教材**, 国内民营连锁医师培训也广泛使用。[T04-S007] [T04-S008] [T04-S009] [T04-S047]

- **Berglundh+Giannobile+Lang+Sanz (eds) 2022 — *Lindhe's Clinical Periodontology and Implant Dentistry* 7th ed** (Wiley-Blackwell). 牙周-种植双线整合教材的事实标准。Jan Lindhe (Gothenburg) 自 1983 1st ed 起持续主编, 7th ed (2022) 由 Tord Berglundh + Giannobile + Niklaus Lang + Mariano Sanz 接棒, 全面整合 2017 World Workshop peri-implant 分类 + 当代再生 + 数字化。两册近 1400 页, **欧洲牙周学 + 种植学 一手教材**, 与 Misch 的"prosthetics-driven 美式"形成"periodontal-biological 欧式"双轨。[T04-S010] [T04-S011]

### ITI / AO / EAO 共识与立场文件 (consensus + position papers)

- **Albrektsson+Zarb+Worthington+Eriksson 1986 — "The long-term efficacy of currently used dental implants: A review and proposed criteria of success"** Int J Oral Maxillofac Implants 1986;1:11-25. **THE 种植成功标准奠基论文**。提出 4 条 success criteria: (a) 个体种植体临床检查无松动; (b) 影像无 peri-implant radiolucency; (c) 一年后骨吸收 <0.2mm/年; (d) 无持续/不可逆 疼痛/感染/神经病变/侵犯下颌管/侵犯上颌窦/侵犯鼻底。**所有后续种植成功定义的引文起点**, 1986-2026 40 年仍是行业基线。[T04-S005]

- **Hämmerle+Chen+Wilson 2004 — "Consensus statements and recommended clinical procedures regarding the placement of implants in extraction sockets"** Int J Oral Maxillofac Implants 19 Suppl. **拔牙后植入时机 Type 1-4 分类奠基**: Type 1 = immediate (拔牙同期), Type 2 = early placement with soft-tissue healing (4-8 wk), Type 3 = early placement with partial bone healing (12-16 wk), Type 4 = late placement (≥6 mo)。Buser 学派的核心引文。[T04-S041]

- **Buser+Chappuis+Belser+Chen 2017 (ITI) — *SAC Assessment Tool* (Straightforward / Advanced / Complex)** (iti.org + Dawson+Chen+Buser+Cordaro+Martin+Belser 2009 *SAC Classification in Implant Dentistry* Quintessence). **病例难度评估的 ITI 共识**, 根据美学风险 + 外科复杂度 + 修复复杂度 把每一个病例分为 S/A/C 三级, 决定是否应转诊或留在原科室。国际接受度极高, 国内 ITI 进修班必教。[T04-S039] [T04-S040]

- **Belser+Grütter+Vailati+Bornstein+Weber+Buser 2009 — "Outcome evaluation of early placed maxillary anterior single-tooth implants using objective esthetic criteria"** J Periodontol。**PES (Pink Esthetic Score) + WES (White Esthetic Score) 当代标准化**。PES 5 项 (mesial/distal papilla, soft tissue level, contour, alveolar process deficiency, color/texture) + WES 5 项, 各 0-2 分; 阈值: PES+WES ≥12/20 视为美学成功。**美学区种植结果评估**的事实标准。原始 PES 来自 Fürhauser et al. 2005。[T04-S042] [T04-S043]

- **Heitz-Mayfield et al. 2018 — "Group 4 ITI Consensus Report: Risks and biologic complications"** Clin Oral Implants Res Suppl。**ITI 2018 风险与生物学并发症共识**, 含 peri-implant disease 风险因素 + 维护频率 + 治疗决策。**临床高频引文**。[T04-S082]

- **Gallucci+Hamilton+Zhou+Buser+Chen 2018 — "Implant placement and loading protocols in partially edentulous patients: A systematic review"** Clin Oral Implants Res Suppl。**ITI 2018 负载 protocol 共识**, 统一 immediate / early / conventional loading 定义。[T04-S083]

- **Jung+Al-Nawas+Araujo et al. 2018 — "Group 1 ITI Consensus Report: Long-term outcomes of implants placed in conjunction with sinus floor elevation"** Clin Oral Implants Res Suppl。**ITI 上颌窦提升长期 outcome 共识** (Bo bone 几何阈值 / 同期 vs 分期 / 侧壁 vs 牙槽嵴顶)。[T04-S051]

- **Lang+Berglundh 2011 — "Peri-implant diseases: where are we now? — Consensus of the Seventh European Workshop on Periodontology"** J Clin Periodontol。**EFP/EAO 2011 peri-implant disease 第一次系统共识**, 与 2017 World Workshop 互为前后参照。[T04-S049]

- **Tonetti+Jung+Avila-Ortiz et al. 2019 — "Management of the extraction socket and timing of implant placement: Consensus report and clinical recommendations of group 3 of the XV European Workshop in Periodontology"** J Clin Periodontol。**EFP 2019 拔牙窝管理 + 种植时机 共识** (含 socket preservation / 拔牙后骨吸收量级 / 影响 implant 决策的解剖阈值)。[T04-S052]

- **ITI Consensus Conference 2018 / 2023** (iti.org)。每 5 年一届的全球种植共识大会, 30+ 学术机构 100+ Fellow 投票通过 4-5 个 workgroup 的 SR + 共识声明, 全部发表在 *Clin Oral Implants Res* Supplement 专刊。**最高级别 ITI 共识来源**。[T04-S061]

- **Academy of Osseointegration (AO)** (osseo.org)。北美种植学最高学术组织, 每年 3 月 Annual Meeting + AO Position Papers (近年涵盖 peri-implantitis 治疗 / All-on-X 适应症 / digital workflow / 颧种植)。[T04-S063]

- **European Association for Osseointegration (EAO)** (eao.org)。欧洲种植学最高学术组织, EAO Congress 每年 10 月, 与 ITI 共识形成欧洲双轨。[T04-S064]

- **International Team for Implantology (ITI)** (iti.org) + **ITI Online Academy** (iti-academy.org)。全球最大的种植学专业组织 (20,000+ Fellow), 出 Treatment Guide + Consensus + Online Academy 教学平台。**国内 ITI 进修班是最主流的国际认证路径**。[T04-S062] [T04-S086]

### 经典 RCT 与系统综述 (classic trials + systematic reviews)

- **Brånemark et al. 1969 — "Intra-osseous anchorage of dental prostheses: I. Experimental studies"** Scand J Plast Reconstr Surg。**Osseointegration 第一篇正式临床论文**, 把 1952 偶然发现 (兔骨骨内固定钛室) 推进到人临床。[T04-S002]

- **Brånemark et al. 1977 — "Osseointegrated implants in the treatment of the edentulous jaw. Experience from a 10-year period"** Scand J Plast Reconstr Surg Suppl 16。**第一个 10 年随访长期数据**, 把"种植"从尝试性疗法转为可接受的标准治疗。[T04-S003]

- **Adell+Lekholm+Rockler+Brånemark 1981 — "A 15-year study of osseointegrated implants in the treatment of the edentulous jaw"** Int J Oral Surg。**15 年长期 outcome 经典**, 上颌 81% / 下颌 91% 5 年成功率 — 行业的第一份硬数据。[T04-S004]

- **Maló+Rangert+Nobre 2003 — "All-on-Four immediate-function concept with Brånemark System implants for completely edentulous mandibles"** Clin Implant Dent Relat Res。**All-on-4 概念奠基论文** (Paulo Maló, Lisbon)。把"4 颗种植体即刻负载支撑全口固定桥"标准化, 两侧远中倾斜植入避开下牙槽神经 + 上颌窦。改写了 1990s 以前需要 6-8 颗种植体 + 6 月骨结合 + 延期负载的 Brånemark protocol。[T04-S035] [T04-S036] [T04-S085]

- **Buser+Janner+Wittneben+Brägger+Ramseier+Salvi 2012 — "10-year survival and success rates of 511 titanium implants with a sandblasted and acid-etched surface"** Clin Oral Implants Res。**Straumann SLA 表面 10 年长期数据奠基**, 95.6% 存活, 89.5% 成功 (按 Albrektsson 1986 严格标准)。SLA 表面成为行业 reference benchmark。[T04-S037] [T04-S038]

- **Tarnow+Cho+Wallace 2000 — "The effect of inter-implant distance on the height of inter-implant bone crest"** J Periodontol。**Tarnow rule of 3** 奠基论文: 相邻种植体间距 ≥3mm 才能保留种植体间骨高度; <3mm 时邻间骨吸收显著增加。影响所有多颗种植规划。[T04-S044]

- **Tarnow+Magner+Fletcher 1992 — "The effect of the distance from the contact point to the crest of bone on the presence or absence of the interproximal dental papilla"** J Periodontol。**papilla 5mm rule** 奠基: 接触点到骨嵴 ≤5mm 时, 龈乳头 100% 存在; 6mm 时 56%; 7mm 时 27%。美学区核心引文。[T04-S045]

- **Salama+Salama+Garber+Adar 1998 — "The interproximal height of bone: a guidepost to predictable aesthetic strategies and soft tissue contours in anterior tooth replacement"** Pract Periodontics Aesthet Dent / J Periodontol。**Salama 美学骨嵴指南**, Maurice & Henry Salama (Atlanta) 兄弟把 Tarnow 规则扩展到种植体 ↔ 邻牙之间的关系。[T04-S046] [T04-S090]

- **Misch CE 1990 — "Density of bone: effect on treatment plans, surgical approach, healing, and progressive bone loading"** Int J Oral Implantol。**D1-D4 骨密度分类奠基**: D1 (致密皮质, 下前牙) / D2 (致密多孔皮质 + 致密小梁, 上前牙 + 下后牙) / D3 (薄多孔皮质 + 细小梁, 上后牙) / D4 (无皮质 + 稀疏小梁, 上后牙窦旁)。影响所有种植 surgical protocol 调整。[T04-S047]

- **Lang+Pun+Lau+Li+Wong 2012 — "A systematic review on survival and success rates of implants placed immediately into fresh extraction sockets after at least 1 year"** Clin Oral Implants Res Suppl。**即刻种植 SR**, ITI consensus 用。[T04-S048]

- **Pjetursson et al. 2007 — "A systematic review of the survival and complication rates of fixed partial dentures (FPDs) after an observation period of at least 5 years"** Clin Oral Implants Res。**种植固定桥 5 年 SR**, 修复并发症基线。[T04-S059]

- **Jung+Zembic+Pjetursson+Zwahlen+Thoma 2012 — "Systematic review of the survival rate and the incidence of biological, technical, and aesthetic complications of single crowns on implants reported in studies with a mean follow-up of 5 years"** Clin Oral Implants Res。**单冠 5 年 SR**, 单牙种植 outcome 标准引用。[T04-S060]

- **Heitz-Mayfield+Mombelli 2014 — "The therapy of peri-implantitis: a systematic review"** Int J Oral Maxillofac Implants Suppl。**peri-implantitis 治疗 SR**, 总结手术 vs 非手术 vs 再生 vs explantation 路径。[T04-S057]

- **Derks+Tomasi 2015 — "Peri-implant health and disease. A systematic review of current epidemiology"** J Clin Periodontol。**peri-implant disease 流行病学 SR**: peri-implant mucositis 43% (29-66%); peri-implantitis 22% (1-47%)。**说明 peri-implantitis 比业内估计严重得多**。[T04-S058]

### 反面 canon / counter-canon (anti-pattern + 风险因素诚实复盘)

- **Klokkevold+Han 2007 — "How do smoking, diabetes, and periodontitis affect outcomes of implant treatment?"** Int J Oral Maxillofac Implants Suppl。**风险因素诚实复盘**, Perry Klokkevold (UCLA Periodontics) + Tae-Ju Han 系统综述, 量化 (a) 吸烟 ↑失败率 OR 2.25; (b) 控制不良 DM (HbA1c >7%) ↑失败率; (c) 治疗后的牙周炎史 ↑peri-implantitis 风险 OR 3-4x。**反面 canon**, 用来反制 "种植万能 / 任何人都可以做" 营销。[T04-S056]

- **Cochrane Oral Health Group Esposito 系列 SR (2009-2014)** (cochranelibrary.com)。Marco Esposito (Manchester / Cochrane Oral Health Editor) 主导, 系统综述维护多项种植主题 (软组织管理 / 骨增量 / 上颌窦提升 / loading protocol / 表面处理), **结论常常是 "insufficient evidence"** — 把厂商赞助的小样本 RCT 的偏倚一一指出, 是 evidence-based dentistry 在种植领域的良心。代表性 SR:
  - Esposito 2012 Cochrane "Interventions for replacing missing teeth: management of soft tissues for dental implants"
  - Esposito 2009 Cochrane "horizontal and vertical bone augmentation techniques"
  - Esposito 2010 Cochrane "augmentation procedures of the maxillary sinus"
  
  **反面 canon 第一引用**, 临床医师拿厂商 RCT 数据决策前必查的对照。[T04-S053] [T04-S054] [T04-S055]

- **Derks+Tomasi 2015 流行病学 SR** (上节已列)。**反面 canon 兼用**: peri-implantitis 真实流行率远高于厂商/民营连锁宣传的 "<5%", 实际 22% (1-47%)。**反制即刻负载 + All-on-4 适应症泛化** 的硬数据。[T04-S058]
